Personal Project · Python 2025

Crossflow Turbine Calculator

Python tool that takes site hydraulic measurements (head and flow rate) and outputs full turbine design parameters (runner diameter, blade geometry, and rotational speed) based on Mockmore & Merryfield and Sammartano design equations. Exports 3D geometry compatible with SolidWorks and Blender. Reduces preliminary turbine sizing from hours of hand calculations to under a minute.

  • Python
  • Parametric Design
  • 3D Geometry Export

Hydropower Permit Tool

Small hydropower developers typically spend days mapping a site's permitting landscape across FERC, state agencies, and environmental rules. This tool cuts that to minutes: enter site characteristics and get applicable regulatory pathways (FERC exemptions, state 401 certifications), flagged obstacles, and a feasibility summary report.

Entrepreneurship 2018 – 2024

Owner & Operator, Landscaping Services

Growing up, I helped my dad with his landscaping business and picked up the trade as I went. Watching him run something he built himself stuck with me.

At 18, I started doing my own residential landscaping work. I handled everything: scheduling, the books, talking with clients, and keeping the equipment running. When a mower gave out, I'd pull up the manual, figure out what was wrong, and get it back together. I ran it for six years, and it paid my way to college.

In June 2024, I closed it down and started at Cal Poly SLO full-time for mechanical engineering. A lot of what I learned running that business came with me into the classroom and the lab: the work ethic, problem-solving, figuring things out on my own.
